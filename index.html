Wire Rope Design (Web Application)

Same “notes style” equations:
W(kgf)=100×W(kN), Wd=W×n×k, Fu=Cu×d², Wr=Cw×d²×L, dw=Cdw×d, D=Cd×d, Ar=Car×d²,
σb=Er(dw/D), Fb=σb×Ar, a=(v1/60)/t, Facc=(Fa/g)×a, Feq=Fa+Fb+Facc, Kw=Fu/Feq
