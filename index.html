Calculation Steps (Important)

This section explains the logic clearly (good for explaining to the doctor and class).
  1. Convert load to kgf (notes style): W = 100 × W(kN)
  2. Design load: Wd = W × n × k
  3. Required diameter from strength: d_calc = √(Wd / Cu)
  4. Rope weight: Wr = Cw × d² × L
  5. Axial load: Fa = W + Wr
  6. Acceleration: a = (v1/60) / t
  7. Bending stress + bending load: dw=Cdw×d, D=Cd×d, Ar=Car×d², σb=Er(dw/D), Fb=σb×Ar
  8. Acceleration force: Facc = (Fa/g) × a
  9. Equivalent load: Feq = Fa + Fb + Facc
  10. Ultimate load: Fu = Cu × d²
  11. Safety factor: Kw = Fu / Feq
  12. Decision: If d_adopted ≥ d_calc → SAFE, else NOT SAFE.
